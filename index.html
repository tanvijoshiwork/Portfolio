About

I work at the intersection of people, purpose, and perception.

By title, I lead Corporate Affairs and Employee Engagement at a listed manufacturing company. In practice, I connect dots. Between leadership and employees. Between ESG commitments and meaningful action. Between what a company does and how it tells that story.

My sweet spot sits between strategy and storytelling. I focus on the details most overlook, because that is where trust is built and reputations are shaped.

A big part of how I work was shaped in London, where I moved when I was 17 with more ambition than certainty. Living independently in a city abroad where no one knew me forced me to figure out who I was without the safety net of familiarity. It sharpened my awareness, taught me to stay grounded while everything around me shifted, and proved that confidence doesn't need to be loud. Those years were more about building myself from scratch in a place that didn't hand out second chances than the degree itself.

Today, I design strategic frameworks, streamline processes, coordinate ESG initiatives, and build engagement across internal and external stakeholders. I've helped Master Components Limited become Maharashtra's first MSME to achieve Zero Waste certification with DIN SPEC: 91436, grown LinkedIn followings from 100 to 1000+, and turned monthly townhalls into something employees actually look forward to.
